Mackbook M4 Details

The MacBook M4 series, encompassing both the MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models, represents Apple's latest leap in laptop innovation, powered by the advanced M4 chip built on second-generation 3-nanometer technology. The MacBook Air M4, available in 13-inch and 15-inch variants, boasts a fanless, ultra-thin design, a vibrant Liquid Retina display, and up to 18 hours of battery life. It supports up to 32GB of unified memory and can connect to two external 6K displays, enhancing multitasking capabilities . The MacBook Pro M4, offered in 14-inch and 16-inch models, features a Liquid Retina XDR display with up to 1,000 nits brightness, a 12MP Center Stage camera, and up to 24 hours of battery life. It includes three Thunderbolt 4 ports and supports two high-resolution external displays, catering to professionals requiring robust performance . Both models integrate Apple's latest AI capabilities through Apple Intelligence, enhancing user experience with advanced machine learning features.
